About the Avengers

The Avengers are Earth’s Mightiest Heroes, a team of extraordinary individuals assembled to protect the world from threats too great for any one hero to handle. Founded by Nick Fury and brought together by Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, the Avengers have faced cosmic enemies, alien invasions, and challenges from within.

Over time, the team expanded to include heroes like Spider-Man, Doctor Strange, Black Panther, Captain Marvel, Vision, and Scarlet Witch. Together, they represent courage, unity, and hope for all of humanity.

Their motto: “Avengers Assemble!”
